# KasbYar Product Requirements Document

## Product Summary
KasbYar is a business operating system for Iranian companies.
It combines CRM, finance, operations, automation, and AI assistance into one platform.

## Primary Users
- Founder / CEO
- Manager
- Sales team
- Finance team
- Operations team
- Support team
- Super admin

## Core Jobs To Be Done
- Understand company status at a glance
- Track customers and deals
- Issue and follow invoices
- Assign and follow tasks
- Automate repetitive workflows
- Ask questions about business memory
- Receive forecasts and recommendations

## MVP Scope
- Authentication and RBAC
- Multi-tenant base structure
- CRM basics
- Invoice basics
- Dashboard
- Audit logs
- Business memory foundation
- AI assistant foundation
- Workflow automation foundation

## Future Scope
- AI CEO
- Digital twin of company
- Predictive analytics
- Competitive intelligence
- Meeting assistant
- Agent platform
- Marketplace and plugins
- Industry packs

## Non-Goals for Early Stage
- Over-engineered microservices
- Complex customization without clear demand
- Features without measurable business value

## Success Metrics
- Daily active business users
- Task completion speed
- Invoice creation time
- Conversion from lead to customer
- Retention by tenant
- Automation adoption
- AI assistant usage

## Product UX Principles
- Minimal
- Fast
- Clear hierarchy
- CEO-first dashboards
- Persian-first copy and layout
- Low cognitive load
