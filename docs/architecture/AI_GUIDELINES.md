# AI Guidelines

## AI Philosophy
AI is not a chatbot. AI is a business operator layer.

## Required AI Capabilities
- Summarize
- Search
- Classify
- Recommend
- Forecast
- Plan
- Execute tool-based workflows

## AI Rules
- AI must operate on business context
- AI must have scoped permissions
- AI must use memory responsibly
- AI outputs must be user-safe and actionable
- AI actions must be traceable

## AI Surfaces
- CEO assistant
- Department agents
- Workflow helpers
- Knowledge retrieval
- Predictive analysis

## Guardrails
- No unsafe tool execution
- No hidden side effects
- No direct exposure of internal reasoning
- No unauthorized data access
