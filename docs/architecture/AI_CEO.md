# AI CEO

## Purpose
The AI CEO is the executive layer that helps a business owner understand status, risks, and actions.

## Responsibilities
- Summarize business health
- Identify anomalies
- Prioritize actions
- Recommend interventions
- Forecast near-term outcomes

## Inputs
- Sales data
- Financial data
- Customer signals
- Task progress
- Automation results
- Business memory

## Outputs
- Daily brief
- Risk list
- Opportunity list
- Recommended decisions
- Follow-up tasks

## Constraints
- It advises and orchestrates
- It does not silently modify important state without permission
